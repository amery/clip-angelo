12.3. Controling classes.

There are three classes for creation and management of dictionaries and depositories:

  • CODBLIST - handles the list of accessible dictionaries.

  • CODBDICTIONARY - handles the content of the dictionary.

  • CODBDEPOSITORY - handles the content of depository.

And one service class:

  • CODBIDLIST - a service class for a work with stored objects in navigating style.
